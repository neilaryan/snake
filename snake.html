Creator: Neil Aryan

Instructions to play:

  1. Use any of the arrows on your keyboard to begin the game.
  2. Use the arrows to move your snake around the board.
  3. Eat as many apples as you can!
  4. Avoid crashing the snake into itself. Don't worry about the borders
  5. When the snake crashes into itself, the size gets reduces to 5 blocks. Enjoy!!
  6. To restart the game, press refresh.
